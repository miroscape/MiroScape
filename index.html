Welcome to MiroScape!

MiroScape is an interactive platform for integrated transcriptomic analyses of glioma. It brings together TCGA bulk RNA-seq, MR3-treated mouse snRNA-seq, and ex vivo treated human tumor samples, enabling users to:

  • Interactively explore cell-type-specific gene expression patterns
  • Compare results across species and sample types
  • Download key gene expression results
  • Investigate conserved transcriptional pathways associated with mitochondrial regulator Miro1 perturbation
